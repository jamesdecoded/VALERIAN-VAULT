How It Works

  1. Place Your Order: Complete checkout with your delivery address
  2. Order Confirmation: Receive confirmation via email and WhatsApp
  3. Processing: We prepare and package your order (1-2 hours)
  4. Dispatch: Your order is dispatched with tracking information
  5. Delivery: Receive your luxury items at your doorstep

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive:

  • WhatsApp notification with tracking details
  • Email with order status updates
  • Real-time delivery updates
  • Delivery partner contact information

Delivery Requirements

Important: Someone must be available to receive the delivery. Valid ID may be required for high-value items.
  • Provide accurate delivery address and phone number
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the package
  • Inspect items upon delivery before signing
  • Report any issues immediately to our support team

Delivery Times

Same-Day Delivery (Nairobi CBD):

  • Orders placed before 12:00 PM - Delivered same day
  • Orders placed after 12:00 PM - Delivered next business day

Standard Delivery:

  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Sunday & Public Holidays: No deliveries

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are calculated based on:

  • Delivery location
  • Order value (Free delivery on orders over KES 50,000 within Nairobi)
  • Delivery speed (same-day, standard)
  • Item size and weight

Failed Delivery

If delivery fails due to:

  • Incorrect address
  • No one available to receive
  • Refused delivery

We will contact you to reschedule. Additional delivery fees may apply for redelivery attempts.
