Authentication Process

Every item goes through our rigorous authentication process:

  1. Source Verification: Confirm item origin from authorized retailers or verified sellers
  2. Physical Inspection: Detailed examination of materials, stitching, hardware, and craftsmanship
  3. Serial Number Check: Verify serial numbers and date codes against brand databases
  4. Logo & Branding: Inspect logos, fonts, and brand markings for accuracy
  5. Packaging Review: Authenticate boxes, dust bags, and accompanying materials
  6. Documentation: Verify receipts, certificates, and authenticity cards
  7. Final Approval: Senior authenticator signs off before listing
