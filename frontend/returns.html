Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority

Return Policy

At VALERIAN VAULT, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy.

14-Day Return Window: You have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Eligibility for Returns

Items must meet the following conditions to be eligible for return:

  • Item is unused and in original condition
  • All original tags, labels, and packaging are intact
  • Item has not been worn, washed, or altered
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)
  • Return initiated within 14 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Worn, used, or damaged items (unless defective)
  • Underwear, swimwear, and intimate apparel (for hygiene reasons)
  • Pierced jewelry and earrings
  • Sale items (unless defective)

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact Us: Reach out via WhatsApp (+254 768 187 542) or email within 14 days
  2. Provide Details: Share your order number and reason for return
  3. Get Authorization: Receive a Return Authorization Number (RAN)
  4. Package Item: Securely pack the item in original packaging
  5. Ship Back: Send to our returns address (provided upon authorization)
  6. Inspection: We inspect the item within 3-5 business days
  7. Refund: Refund processed within 7-10 business days

Return Shipping

Who Pays for Return Shipping?

  • Defective/Wrong Item: We cover return shipping costs
  • Change of Mind: Customer covers return shipping costs
  • Size Exchange: We cover shipping for first exchange

Return Shipping Options

You can return items via:

  • Our courier pickup service (KES 300-500 depending on location)
  • Drop-off at our Nairobi location (address provided)
  • Your preferred courier service

Refund Policy

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Approved Returns: Full refund to original payment method
  • Processing Time: 7-10 business days
  • M-Pesa Refunds: 1-3 business days
  • Card Refunds: 5-10 business days (depends on bank)
  • Notification: Email confirmation when refund is processed
Note: Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for:

  • Different size of the same item
  • Different color of the same item
  • Defective or damaged items

Exchange Process

  1. Contact us to request an exchange
  2. Return the original item following return process
  3. We'll ship the replacement item once we receive the return
  4. First exchange shipping is free (for size/color changes)

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item:

  • Contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Provide photos of the defect or damage
  • We'll arrange free pickup and replacement
  • Full refund or replacement at your choice
  • All shipping costs covered by us
Important: Inspect your items upon delivery and report any issues immediately.

Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours
  • Provide order number and photos
  • We'll arrange free pickup of wrong item
  • Correct item shipped immediately
  • No additional charges

Partial Returns

For orders with multiple items:

  • You can return individual items from your order
  • Each item must meet return eligibility criteria
  • Refund calculated based on returned items
  • Shipping fees may be adjusted proportionally

Cancellations

You can cancel your order:

  • Before Dispatch: Full refund, no questions asked
  • After Dispatch: Follow standard return process
  • How to Cancel: Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email

Instead of a refund, you can opt for:

  • Store credit for future purchases
  • 10% bonus on store credit value
  • No expiration date
  • Can be used on any item
